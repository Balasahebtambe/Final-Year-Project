Our University

We are affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU), also known as UniPune, is one of the premier universities in India. It is approved by the UGC and accredited by NAAC with an ‘A’ grade. The university is popularly known as the 'Oxford of the East'. UniPune has been ranked 19th among top universities in India by NIRF 2023. It has over 700 affiliated colleges, 46 academic departments, and 52 research centres.
